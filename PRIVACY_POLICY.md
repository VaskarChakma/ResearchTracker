Research Paper Tracker does not collect, transmit, or sell personal user data.

All saved research papers, metadata, annotations, tags, and user settings are stored locally within the user's browser using Chrome storage APIs.

The extension accesses publicly available research paper information from supported academic websites only when the user explicitly initiates a save action.

No browsing history is tracked.
No personal information is collected.
No analytics or third-party tracking services are used.
No data is transmitted to external servers.

Users maintain full control over their saved data and may export or delete it at any time.
